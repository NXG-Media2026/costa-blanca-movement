## What Makes Sports Massage Different?

Sports massage goes beyond general relaxation massage. Our therapist uses a combination of techniques tailored to your specific condition:

- **Deep tissue work** — firm pressure to reach deeper muscle layers and release chronic adhesions
- **Myofascial release** — sustained pressure on connective tissue to restore mobility and reduce pain
- **Trigger point therapy** — targeted pressure on specific knots that refer pain to other areas
- **Stretching techniques** — assisted stretching to improve flexibility and range of motion

## Benefits of Regular Sports Massage

Regular sports massage delivers measurable results:

- **Faster recovery** — increased blood flow flushes metabolic waste and delivers nutrients to damaged tissue
- **Reduced muscle tension** — release of chronic tightness that contributes to pain and poor posture
- **Improved flexibility** — greater range of motion reduces injury risk
- **Better circulation** — enhanced blood and lymph flow supports overall healing
- **Pain management** — effective for chronic pain conditions without relying on medication

## Who Benefits from Sports Massage?

Our sports massage therapy is popular with:

- **Active expats** — cyclists, golfers, hikers, swimmers, and tennis players on the Costa Blanca
- **Desk workers** — those suffering from neck, shoulder, and upper back tension
- **Post-injury recovery** — patients rehabilitating from strains, sprains, or surgery
- **Chronic pain sufferers** — people with ongoing back pain, headaches, or repetitive strain injuries

## What to Expect During Your Session

Your first appointment begins with a brief assessment of your condition, movement patterns, and treatment goals. This ensures the massage is precisely targeted to your needs.

During the treatment, you'll lie on a professional massage table in a warm, comfortable treatment room. Sessions typically last 45–60 minutes. Your therapist will check in with you throughout to ensure the pressure is right.

After your massage, we'll recommend stretches or exercises to extend the benefits between sessions.
